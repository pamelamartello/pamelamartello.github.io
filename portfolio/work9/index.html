PAMELA MARTELLO

Mirru in-out

FabAcademy Final Project

Smart cat-door entrance system

In 2019 I joined an amazing adventure in the world of Digital Fabrication the FabAcademy course. It is an intense 20 weeks course where you learn HOW TO MAKE ALMOST ANYTHING.

During this course you have to come up with a final project that integrates as many tools and topics learnt throughout the FabAcademy.

For my final project I decided to make something for our Shanghainese cat Mirru. The idea is for her to access our home with a special key (a special necklace with a tag) that enables the opening only to her and no other cat/animal can enter her/our house. The entrance is not only a door but a shelter for her where she can rest, chill or protect from the weather. For this project I made most of the parts and systems by myself, using from 3D printing, laser cutting, CNC milling, PCB design and programming, molding and casting, lots of patience and research.
